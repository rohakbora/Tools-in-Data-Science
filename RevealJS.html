Attribution Model (Code Sample)

# Simple contribution margin calc for segments
segments = {
    "Retail": {"rev": 1_800_000_000, "direct_cost": 1_240_000_000},
    "Corporate": {"rev": 1_200_000_000, "direct_cost": 840_000_000},
    "Markets": {"rev": 420_000_000, "direct_cost": 260_000_000},
}
margin = {k: v["rev"] - v["direct_cost"] for k, v in segments.items()}
print({k: f"${m:,.0f}" for k, m in margin.items()})

Financial Math

Net Present Value (NPV):

\[\mathrm{NPV} = \sum_{t=1}^{T} \frac{CF_t}{(1+r)^t} - C_0\]

Capital Asset Pricing Model (CAPM):

\[\mathbb{E}[R_i] = r_f + \beta_i\big(\mathbb{E}[R_m] - r_f\big)\]

Briefly remind audience why discount rate matters. For CAPM, link back to risk disclosures and WACC used in guidance.
